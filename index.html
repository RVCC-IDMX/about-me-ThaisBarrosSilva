CSS animation

A web page is the web developer's voice in the crowd, for it to be understood and heard the web developer must communicate in an appropriate way to the reader. Delivering content to the customer goes beyond putting information on a page, it must be well located and aligned for good organization and visualization and with attributes to draw the listener's attention.

Everything starts by inserting an image in the html; once decided, it is at that moment that the functions of Cascading Style Sheets or better known as CSS are present in this journey. A good image can be configured in several ways and use Animation to make it more attractive to the customer.

My first step was to add the picture on HTML

Then I gave the animation for it. I used : transition-duration: 1000ms and transition-timing-function: ease-in-out;

After this process I added transparence because my main idea was not change any color in this animation, also I added the dimension of rotation using:
background :transparent;
transform: rotate(35deg);

After adding these four possible characteristics for CSS transporations the image was rotated by 35 deg. with a duration of 100ms and with the ease-in-out function, that is basically the transition starts quickly and gradually slows down until the end

I created a website with the proposal to transmit elegance, minimalism and organization. I hope you like it!
